    research

    I am broadly interested in Latin American Literary and Cultural Studies. My research mainly focuses on Mesoamerican Literature and Culture, especifically on indigenous language texts and how they are affected by and respond to colonialism and coloniality. Additionally, I work on the development of pedagogically sound materials and methods for Indigenous Language instruction, especially in K’iche’.

    Other areas of interest include: Indigenous Studies, Religious Studies, Evangelization, Poetry, Translation, Central American Literature, Less-Commonly-Taught Languages.
